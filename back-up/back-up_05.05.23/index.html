classic massage

With a technical performance, massage allows you to get rid of local pain and swelling, cosmetic defects, as well as improve the condition of individual organs and systems.

Indications for massage

  • The recovery period after injuries or operations on the spine
  • Osteochondrosis, radiculitis, arthritis or other diseases of the musculoskeletal system
  • Curvature of the spine - lordosis, kyphosis, scoliosis
  • Chronic diseases of the digestive, respiratory, and circulatory systems
  • Decrease in immunity and work capacity, weakness and apathy
  • Increased nervousness, frequent stress or other signs of nervous system disorders

Benefits of massage

  • Rapid disappearance of back pain and other symptoms of osteochondrosis, radiculitis and other spine diseases.
  • Improvement of general well-being, improvement of immunity, comprehensive improvement of the body.
  • Relatively low prices for therapeutic back massage. The procedure is cheaper than expensive drugs, but very effective.
  • Proven benefit and absence of complications. If the massage is performed by a qualified specialist, the risk of unwanted consequences is minimized.

Contraindications for the purpose of massage

  • Acute fever and high fever
  • Bleeding and tendency to it
  • Purulent processes of any localization
  • Allergic diseases with skin rashes
  • Mental illness with excessive arousal
  • 3rd and 4th degree circulatory failure
